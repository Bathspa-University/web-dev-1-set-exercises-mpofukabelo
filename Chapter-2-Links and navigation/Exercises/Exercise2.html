Overview

Interstellar is a science fiction film directed by Christopher Nolan, released in 2014. The film follows a group of astronauts who travel through a wormhole near Saturn in search of a new habitable planet for humanity.

The film stars Matthew McConaughey, Anne Hathaway, Jessica Chastain, Michael Caine, and others.

Plot

In the near future, Earth is facing environmental collapse, and humanity's survival is at stake. Former NASA pilot Cooper, along with a team of scientists, embarks on a journey through space to find a new home for humanity.

Characters

  • Cooper (Matthew McConaughey) - A former NASA pilot and the protagonist of the film.
  • Dr. Brand (Anne Hathaway) - A scientist and member of the space exploration team.
  • Murph (Jessica Chastain) - Cooper's daughter, who plays a significant role in the plot.
  • Professor Brand (Michael Caine) - The head of NASA and Dr. Brand's father.

Reviews

Interstellar received critical acclaim for its visuals, performances, and ambitious storytelling. It was praised for its scientific accuracy and emotional depth.
